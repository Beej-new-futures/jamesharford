## Little Simz: Tour Visuals

**Role/Credit:** Design, Direction, Animation

Little Simz reached out to Beej through Jeremy Cole to design her tour visuals for her track 'Speed' from the album 'Sometimes I might be introvert'

Beej designed and animated for the tracks performance on the Jimmy Kimmel show.

See the animation below

Made in Adobe Suite and Cinema 4d
