## Mizuno : Brand Film

**Role/Credit:** Director, CGI, Full post-production

Beej was called into Mizuno Europe to direct and create a campaign film for the release of their running shoe Wave Rider 25. This was an epic amount of cg renders and tonnes of fun.

Here are some of the key images from the campaign.

Created with Cinema 4d and Octane render
