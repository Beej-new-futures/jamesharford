Little Simz Performance visuals: The Brits

Director / Concept / Design & Animation

Little Simz reached out to Beej through J Cole to design and deliver stage visuals for her performance of 'Introvert' and 'Woman' at the 2022 Brit Awards

Working alongside jeremy Cole to build and animate a storm scene for Intro vert, and a 50 jazz club, lux set for 'Woman'

She went on to win Best New Artist - and IMO she should have won 'Best Album' as well. The project was a lot of fun to work on. Shout out to the team.

All made with Cinema 4d and After Effects.
